Chicken Adobo

Description

Adobo, a beloved Filipino dish worldwide, can use pork or chicken, but this recipe features a soy sauce-filled chicken version.

This classic dish combines tender chicken with savory soy sauce, vinegar, and spices for a rich, flavorful meal.

Ingredients

(Taken from panlasang pinoy)

  • 2 lbs chicken
  • pieces dried bay leaves
  • tablespoons soy sauce
  • tablespoons white vinegar
  • garlic
  • 1/2 cups water
  • tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • teaspoon whole peppercorn (note 8)
  • Parsley or leeks * optional for garnish

Steps:

  1. Combine chicken, soy sauce, and garlic in a large bowl. Mix well. Marinate the chicken for at least 1 hour. Note: the longer the time, the better 2 lbs chicken, 4 tablespoons soy sauce.
  2. Heat a cooking pot. Pour cooking oil. 3 tablespoons cooking oil.
  3. When the oil is hot enough, pan-fry the marinated chicken for 2 minutes per side.
  4. Pour-in the remaining marinade, including garlic. Add water. Bring to a boil 1 1/2 cups water.
  5. Add dried bay leaves and whole peppercorn. Simmer for 30 minutes or until the chicken gets tender 3 pieces dried bay leaves, 1 teaspoon whole peppercor.
  6. Add vinegar. Stir and cook for 10 minutes. 6 tablespoons white vinegar.
  7. Put-in the sugar, and salt. Stir and turn the heat off.Serve hot. Share and Enjoy! 1 teaspoon sugar, 1/4 teaspoon salt.
